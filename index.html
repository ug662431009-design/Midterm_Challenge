Hebron

Hebron is known for its old city and rich heritage.

jenin

jenin is a city in the West Bank of Palestine. It serves as an economic and commercial center for the northern part of the West Bank.

Jericho

Jericho is located near the Jordan River and is known for its fertile land and oases, making it historically important for agriculture.

Nablus

Nablus is located north of Jerusalem and is one of the largest cities in the West Bank.

Qalqilya

Qalqilya is located in the northwestern part of the West Bank, near the border with Israel.

Quds

Al-Quds (Jerusalem) is One of the world’s oldest and holiest cities, sacred to Islam (Al-Aqsa Mosque, Dome of the Rock), Christianity (Church of the Holy Sepulchre), and Judaism (Western Wall). It is culturally, historically, and politically central for both Palestinians and Israelis.

Ramallah

Ramallah is a city in the central West Bank of Palestine.It serves as the administrative and political center of the Palestinian Authority.

Deir al-Balah

Deir al-Balah is a city in the central Gaza Strip of Palestine.

Gaza

Gaza (Gaza City) is the largest city in the Gaza Strip, Palestine.

Khan Yunis

Khan Yunis is a city in the southern Gaza Strip of Palestine.It is the second largest city in Gaza after Gaza City.

Rafah

Rafah is a city in the southern Gaza Strip, right on the border with Egypt.

Tulkarm

Tulkarm is a city in the northwestern part of the West Bank, Palestine, near the border with Israel.
